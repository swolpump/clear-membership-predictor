Methodology & Key Findings

Best Model: CLEAR Revenue + TSA Daily Passengers + Seasonality (Ridge Regression)

Backtesting: Leave-One-Out Cross-Validation across 20 quarters (Q1 2021 – Q4 2025). R² = 0.9395, MAE = 364k members, MAPE = 8.86%.

Key Insight: CLEAR's own revenue is the single strongest predictor (r=0.967), but combining it with TSA passenger throughput and a seasonal indicator yields the best out-of-sample accuracy. The negative seasonality coefficient (-259k) means Q2/Q3 membership is slightly lower than revenue alone would suggest — likely because peak-season sign-ups include more short-duration trial memberships that churn.

Risk Factors: TSA's free touchless ID rollout could cannibalize CLEAR+ value proposition. Gross dollar retention declined from 88.5% to 86.4% in 2025. Credit card travel spending turned negative in H2 2025.

Growth Deceleration: YoY growth has fallen from 40%+ (2022-2023) to single digits (2024-2025). The model projects a modest reacceleration to ~12% in 2026, driven by continued revenue expansion and stable travel volumes.
